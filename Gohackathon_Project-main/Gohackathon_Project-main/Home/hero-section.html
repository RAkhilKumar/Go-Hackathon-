About GoHackathon

Go Hackathon is a platform that brings together industry professionals, educators, and students to foster talent and innovation. It aims to equip students with advanced skills, software, and industry best practices, and provides a ecosystem for industry-academia partnership to develop impactful ideas.

Through a series of events, seminars, hackathons, internships, and projects, Go Hackathon offers nurturing, mentorship, skills, and professional opportunities to its members and participants.

FAQs

How can I register for a webinar or training workshop?

Register on our website following the event page instructions for easy sign-up.

What topics are covered in the webinars and training workshops?

Our events cover a variety of topics, including industry trends, personal development, and specialized skills training.

Are the webinars and training workshops free to attend?

Most events are free, although some may require a registration fee for specialized workshops or premium content.

How do I access the webinar or training workshop on the day of the event?

Simply log in to your account at the scheduled time to access the live session.

Will recordings be available for the webinars and training workshops?

Yes, recordings are typically provided after the event for participants to review at their convenience.
