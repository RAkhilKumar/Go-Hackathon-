Job description

As a Hackathon Coordinator, you will be responsible for planning, organizing, and executing our hackathon events. You will work closely with cross-functional teams to ensure each event is successful, from initial concept through post-event analysis. This role requires a blend of project management, event coordination, and community engagement skills.

Job requirements

  • Bachelor's degree in Event Management, Marketing, Business Administration, Computer Science, or a related field.
  • Minimum of 2 years of experience in event planning and coordination, preferably in the tech industry or with hackathons.
  • Demonstrated experience managing events with 100+ participants.
  • Proven track record of working with sponsors, vendors, and partners to secure resources and support.
