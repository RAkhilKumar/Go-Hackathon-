4P Programs

The 4P Program by GoHackathon is an innovative and structured initiative designed to equip individuals with essential skills for a successful career in the tech industry. It focuses on four key pillars—Placement, Prepare, Profession, and Project—ensuring a comprehensive learning experience that blends knowledge, practical application, and career advancement.

Whether you are a student, fresher, or professional looking to upskill, the 4P Program provides the perfect roadmap to a successful tech career.
